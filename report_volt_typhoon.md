## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Volt Typhoon is a hacking group from China that attacks U.S. power plants, water systems, airports, and internet companies. They get inside these systems and stay hidden for years, waiting to cause damage if needed.

**Threat Level:** CRITICAL  
**Status:** Currently Active

---

## WHO IS VOLT TYPHOON?

**Name:** Volt Typhoon  
**Also Known As:** Vanguard Panda, BRONZE SILHOUETTE  
**Country:** China  
**Organization:** Ministry of State Security (MSS)  
**Team Size:** 200-500 people  
**Budget:** $50-100 million per year

---

## WHAT DO THEY ATTACK?

They target critical U.S. infrastructure:

1. **Power Plants** - Electricity generation and distribution
2. **Water Systems** - Water treatment and dams
3. **Transportation** - Airports, ports, railroads
4. **Communications** - Phone companies and internet infrastructure
5. **Guam** - U.S. military location in Pacific

---
